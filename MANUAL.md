# Vynta User Manual 📘

Welcome to **Vynta**, your AI-powered productivity architect. This guide will help you master the interface and get the most out of your experience.

## 1. Getting Started
When you first open Vynta, you will be prompted to sign in with your **Google Account**. This allows Vynta to sync with your Google Calendar so you can see your existing events alongside your new AI-generated tasks.

## 2. Navigation
Vynta uses a **Persistent Dock** at the bottom of the screen:
- **Home Icon**: Your daily agenda.
- **History Icon**: Review your past accomplishments.
- **Profile/Architect (in Settings)**: Learn about the creator.

## 3. Adding Tasks with AI 🧠
This is the heart of Vynta. Instead of filling out long forms, just talk to it:
1. Tap the **Mic** button or the **Input Field**.
2. Speak or type naturally: *"I need to write the project report tomorrow at 10 AM, it's high priority and I'll need a lot of energy."*
3. Vynta will automatically extract:
   - **Task Name**: "Write project report"
   - **Time**: Tomorrow, 10:00 AM
   - **Priority**: High (4/5)
   - **Energy Level**: High

## 4. Energy-Based Productivity
Vynta categorizes tasks by **Energy Levels**:
- ⚡ **High Energy**: Complex tasks (Coding, Writing, Strategy).
- 🔋 **Medium Energy**: Routine tasks (Emails, Meetings).
- 🧘 **Low Energy**: Admin or light tasks (Filing, Organization).

Use the **Filter** chips on the Home screen to view tasks that match your current mental state.

## 5. Managing Your Agenda
- **Complete a Task**: Tap the checkbox or task card. You'll feel a satisfying haptic pulse.
- **View Details**: Tap any task to see its AI-generated breakdown.
- **Delete**: Long-press or use the delete icon in the task detail view.

## 6. Personalization
Go to **Settings** to:
- Toggle between **Light** and **Dark** modes.
- Sign out or switch accounts.
- View the **Architect** screen to see the neural-designed developer profile.
